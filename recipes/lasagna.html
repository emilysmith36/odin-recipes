lasagna

ingredients

  • 1 lb sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minched onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 28 oz crushed toamtoes
  • 6.5 oz canned tomato sauce
  • 6 oz tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 4 tbsp chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 lb mozarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

directions

step 1

gather ingredients

step 2

cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned

step 3

stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. season with sugar, 2 tbsp of parsley, basil, 1tsp salt, Italian season, fennel seeds, and pepper. simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occassionally

step 4

bring large pot of lightly salted water to boil. cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8-10mins. drain noodles, rinse with cold water.

step 5

in mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tbsp of parsley and 1/2 tsp salt

step 6

preheat oven to 375 degrees F

step 7

spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in bottom of 9x13 inch baking dish. arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. spread 1/2 ricotta cheese mixture. top with 1/3 of mozarella slices. spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozarella, sprinkle with 1/4 cup parmasen cheese.

step 8

repeat layers, top with remaining mozarella and parmesan cheese. cover with foil, and spray foil with cooking spray or make sure foil does not touch cheese in order to prevent sticking.

step 9

bake in preheated oven for 25mins. remove foil and bake for additional 25mins

step 10

rest lasagna for 15mins before serving
